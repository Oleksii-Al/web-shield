Ransomware is a dangerous type of malware that locks your files and demands a ransom to get them back. Fortunately, there are simple steps you can take to protect yourself from becoming a victim. Follow these tips to stay safe and keep your data secure.

  • Back up your important files regularly and store them offline.
  • Keep your software and apps updated to fix any security holes.
  • Be careful with email attachments and links, especially from unknown senders.
  • Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your accounts.
  • Install trusted antivirus software to block threats.
  • Limit access to sensitive files and don't give out unnecessary permissions.
  • Turn off remote access features if you don't need them.
